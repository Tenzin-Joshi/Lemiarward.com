Rules / ルール

1. Respect every member of the group at all times.

2. Active participation in group activities is encouraged.

3. Any decision taken by the group majority should be respected and approved by all.

4. Maintain a positive environment and encourage each other.

5. There is no ruler/owner/head at all, all decisions will depend on the majority of members

6. Any member can propose a new rule or change in existing one if the worst majority agrees

7. All members must not break any of the terms and conditions and the expulsion terms and conditions rules. If any member is caught doing so they shall be expelled after a majority votes at the next lemiarward group discussion.


if a member is being a constant menace to the majority, then his/her expulsion will be taken into account at the next meetup / メンバーが多数派にとって常に脅威である場合、そのメンバーの除名は次回の交流会で考慮されます。

Regulations / 規則

1. Meetings should be attended regularly unless previously excused.

2. Avoid spamming the group chat or derailing conversations.

3. Group funds, if any, should be managed transparently by the assigned treasurer.

4. All financial funds should be managed by the assigned treasurer and should be kept hidden to all members, exept their own funds.

5. If your funds are mismanaged / not able to return, then the assigned treasurer will be responsible and punished depending on the severity of the problem . Punishments will be decided at the next meetup by the group.

Terms & Conditions / 利用規約

1. Membership can be revoked if any member is found violating the rules consistently.

2. All personal details of members should be kept confidential within the group.

3. Decisions on significant matters require a unanimous or majority vote.

4. This webpage wll be updated whenever a new rule is added or an existing one is changed, or when a member joins or is expelled.

5. This website will be updated by Tenzin Joshi

6. The basic changes will all be managed by Tenzin Joshi

Joining Conditions / 加入条件

1. The new member must be approved by all other existing members

2. The new member must be learning Japanese in duolingo

3. The new member must be active in the group chat

4. The new member must have more than a 20 day learning streak in duolingo at the current time of joining

5. The new member must be willing to participate in group activities

6. The new member must be over unit 2 in section 1 in duolingo

7. The new member must be willing to help others in the group

8. The new member must have more than 2000xp in their duolingo account

8. The new member will have to attend 1 lemiarward meetup after completing all of the above conditions to finally become a new member.

9. The new member must agree to all the terms and conditions, rules, regulations, and expulsion terms and conditions, otherwise, the member shall not be allowed to join the group

10. The new member can be unable to join if one of the agreeing member later disagrees for joining permission


No shortcut exeptions can be made and if a new member is selected to join then IT IS COMPULSORY TO FILL ALL THE CONDITIONS. If a member fails to do even one then they are restricted to join until all the criterias are completed..
NO EXEPTIONS ARE GOING TO BE MADE FOR ANYBODY AND DUOLINGO ACCOUNT SCREENSHOTS MUST BE VERIFIED BY ALL OFFICIAL MEMBERS TO PROOVE THE COMPLETION OF SOME OF THE CRITERIAS..

Expulsion T&C / 除名規約

1. Any member who is found to be violating the rules consistently will be expelled from the group

2. Any member disrespecting, discriminating, or bullying another member will be expelled from the group

3. Any member constantly violating the Rules, Regulations, Terms & Conditions with multiple prior warnings will be expelled from the group at the discretion of the group majority

Any member opposed for expulsion by the vast majority at a lemiarward meetup will be expelled soon after
